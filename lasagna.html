Lasagna

This Lasagna recipe has ranked among the most popular lasagna recipes on the internet for more than 20 years.

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but time consuming lasagna is the reward.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasgana noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the Lasagna rest before serving.
