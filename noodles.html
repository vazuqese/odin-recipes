Creamy Peanut Butter Jackfruit Noodles

Noodles

This is a quick noodle dish that is loaded with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cups canned young green jackfruit, roughly chopped
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 14 oz pre-cooked udon noodles
  • 1 stalk green onion, sliced, light and dark green parts separated

Steps

  1. To a saute pan on medium-high heat, cook the bell pepper and jackfruit in the oil for 3-4 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, combine the sauce ingredients in a jar.
  3. Add the udon noodles and the white and light green parts of the green onion to the pan. Gently break the noodles apart, cooking for 2 minutes.
  4. Pour the sauce into the pan and cook until everything is well coated in sauce.
  5. Garnish with the dark green parts of the green onion, and add any other desired toppings. Enjoy!
